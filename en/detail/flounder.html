Flounder

A kind of flatfish similar to the plaice and dab.

Sources:

The Monterey Bay Aquarium lists flounders as a mixture of Good Alternative and Avoid, while the MCS rates them as a 3. On balance, flounder not known to be specifically sustainable should be avoided.
