Cockle (Dredged)

Small saltwater clams with a round, ribbed shell. Avoid cockles harvested by dredging as this damages the seafloor.

Sources:

  • Marine Conservation Society

The MCS advises against consuming dredged cockles as dredging damages the sea floor. Hand-gathered cockles are rated 2. The Monterey Bay Aquarium, FishWatch and Greenpeace have no data.
