Skate

Cartilaginous fish with "wings" and a long, thin tail. They are severely overfished and in decline.

Sources:

The Monterey Bay Aquarium rates MSC certified skates as a "Good Alternative" but all others "Avoid", and the MCS rate all skates as 5. Skates are on the Greenpeace red list.
