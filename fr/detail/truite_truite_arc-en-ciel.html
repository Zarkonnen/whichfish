Truite (Truite arc-en-ciel)

A farmed freshwater fish with reddish flesh that is a popular alternative to salmon.

Monterey Bay Aquarium states that farmed rainbow trout are a good choice. The MCS rate farmed rainbow trout as 1-3 on a scale of 5, with 1 for organic farmed trout. FishWatch and Greenpeace have no data.
