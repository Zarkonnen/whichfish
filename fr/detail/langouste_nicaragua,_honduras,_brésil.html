Langouste (Nicaragua, Honduras, Brésil)

The Monterey Bay Aquarium states that spiny lobster from Mexico is sustainable, and US and Caribbean lobster is OK, while lobster from Nicaragua, Honduras and Brazil is rated as "Avoid". Lobster fisheries in Nicaragua, Honduras and Brazil are plagued with issues of overfishing, catching juveniles, and illegal fishing.
