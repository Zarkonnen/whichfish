Saumon (Coho / Saumon Argenté US/Canadien)

The Monterey Bay Aquarium lists farmed US Coho as a Best Choice and wild Canadian Coho as a good alternative.

Sources:

  • FDA Mercury Table

The MCS lists wild-caught salmon as a 5, and rates most farmed salmon as a 3. The Monterey Bay Aquarium lists the same farmed salmon as Avoid, making it not recommended, but does specifically mention Alaskan and Coho as good options.
