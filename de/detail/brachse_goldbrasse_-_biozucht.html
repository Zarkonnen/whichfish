Brachse (Goldbrasse - Biozucht)

Also called daurade or dorada, a popular Mediterranean fish that is unfortunately farmed in an environmentally damaging way - but organic certified Gilthead Bream is fine according to the MCS.

The MCS rates organic farmed gilthead bream as a 2, but other kinds of bream as a 4-5. The Monterey Bay Aquarium lists gilthead farmed in closed tanks as a Best Choice.
